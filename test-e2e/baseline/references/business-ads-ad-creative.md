 Placements 

 The places where you can run your ads are called Placements. You choose where your ads appear - on Facebook, Messenger, WhatsApp or Instagram. This depends on the objective you choose when you create your campaign, as well as your ad type and settings. We recommend selecting 6 or more placements. Keep in mind that not all placements are available for every ad objective.
