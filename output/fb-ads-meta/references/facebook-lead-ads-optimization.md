# Facebook Lead Ads Optimization Guide

Source: https://www.wordstream.com/blog/how-to-optimize-facebook-lead-ads

## 5 Core Optimization Strategies

### 1. Ad Text and Creative

**For Higher Volume:**
- Engaging visuals, high-contrast colors
- Test various copy lengths
- Broadly appealing messaging
- Social proof and industry awards

**For Better Quality:**
- Specify target groups in messaging
- Use ideal customer persona imagery
- Narrow offer to serious buyers only
- Qualify leads visually and verbally

### 2. Form Type Selection

- **More Volume Forms**: Quick, mobile-friendly, no review step
- **Higher Intent Forms**: Review step + manual slide to submit, deters casual clicks

### 3. Form Introduction Section

- Set expectations about what users receive post-submission
- For volume: welcoming language or omit greeting
- For quality: prequalify prospects in greeting

### 4. Form Questions Strategy

**For Volume:**
- Prioritize prefill questions (auto-populated from profiles)
- Minimize total questions
- Request only essentials (name, phone, email)

**For Quality:**
- Custom questions requiring thoughtful responses
- Detailed questions about budget, timeline, needs
- Conditional logic to branch forms
- Include opt-out options to filter unmotivated leads

### 5. Lead Follow-Up

- Contact within 24-48 hours
- Align follow-up with initial CTA
- Remove inactive prospects to preserve resources
