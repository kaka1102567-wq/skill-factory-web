# Why Facebook Ads Aren't Converting: 9 Reasons & Fixes

Source: https://www.wordstream.com/blog/ws/2023/06/23/facebook-ads-not-converting

## 1. Unoptimized Landing Page
Ensure messaging consistency between ad and landing page. Test speed and mobile. Make conversion actions clear.

## 2. Meta Pixel Setup Issues
Review conversion events in Events Manager. Address flagged tracking issues.

## 3. Unclear Ad Copy
Include power words and clear calls-to-action. Explicitly state desired conversion outcome.

## 4. Wrong Audience Targeting
Conduct deeper audience research. Use creative targeting connecting interests indirectly to offering.

## 5. Ad Fatigue
Monitor frequency metrics. Refresh ads when frequency reaches 5-10. Rotate copy, creative, CTAs. Diversify targeting.

## 6. Misaligned Campaign Objective
Select objectives matching business goals. A/B test different objectives.

## 7. Insufficient Budget
Ensure budgets align with average CPC metrics. Maintain flexibility in spending.

## 8. Insufficient Time to Convert
Allow 2+ weeks for algorithm learning. Wait 30 days before making decisions. Account for longer sales cycles.

## 9. Indirect Conversions (Uncredited)
Facebook ads may influence conversions on other platforms. Monitor cross-channel reporting. Set up offline conversion tracking.
