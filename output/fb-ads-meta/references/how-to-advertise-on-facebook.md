# How to Advertise on Facebook: Complete Guide

Source: https://www.wordstream.com/blog/how-to-advertise-on-facebook

## Why Advertise on Facebook?

- Visual branding power: Unlike Google Ads (all text), Facebook allows images and video
- Cost efficiency: Average CPC $1.88 for lead campaigns, $0.77 for traffic campaigns
- Full-funnel capability: Multiple ad formats and objectives for every marketing stage
- Nearly 90% of businesses use Facebook advertising
- Users click approximately 11 ads monthly on average

## Account Structure

- Meta Business Suite: Umbrella account managing Facebook and Instagram
- Ads Manager: Where ad accounts and campaigns live
- Campaign hierarchy: Campaign → Ad Set → Ad

## 8-Step Process to Launch Facebook Ads

### Step 1: Create Business Suite Account
Visit business.facebook.com with business name, personal name, email.

### Step 2: Connect or Create Facebook Page
Settings > Business Settings > Pages. Build out Page details before advertising.

### Step 3: Create Ad Account
Business Settings > Ad Accounts. Set name, timezone, currency.

### Step 4: Install Facebook Pixel
Data Sources > Pixels > Add. Choose partner integration (WordPress, Shopify, WooCommerce) or manual. Tracks visitor behavior post-click.

### Step 5: Create Campaign
Open Ads Manager > Create. Choose from 6 objectives: awareness, traffic, engagement, leads, app promotion, sales. Choose Advantage+ (automated) or manual control.

### Step 6: Configure Ad Set
- Daily budgets: for evergreen campaigns, start $5-10/day minimum
- Lifetime budgets: for time-limited campaigns with fixed spend
- Audience targeting: Core audiences, Custom audiences, Lookalike audiences, Advantage+ audiences
- Placements: Begin with automatic/Advantage+, optimize manually after 1-2 weeks

### Step 7: Create Ad Creative
- Formats: single image/video, carousel, collection ads
- Primary text: first 125 characters show
- Headline: 40 characters max
- Description: 30 characters max
- Perfect Facebook ad headline length is five words

### Step 8: Publish
Facebook reviews ads (24+ hours). Ads enter learning period.

## Ad Auction Mechanics

Success depends on: (1) Your bid amount, (2) Ad quality and relevance, (3) Estimated action rates. Higher quality and relevance = better positioning without increasing bids.

## Critical Setup Elements

- Pixel + Conversions API for complete data visibility
- Ad scheduling matching audience behavior
- Frequency controls to prevent ad fatigue
- Dynamic creative testing with multiple asset combinations

## Performance Optimization Tips

- Start with lower daily budgets to gather data
- Don't over-target multiple interests (1-2 per ad set)
- A/B test emotional appeals
- After 1-2 weeks, switch to manual placement optimization
- Respect learning period; avoid frequent changes
