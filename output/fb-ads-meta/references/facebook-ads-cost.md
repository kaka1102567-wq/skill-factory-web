# How Much Do Facebook Ads Cost? (2025 Benchmarks)

Source: https://www.wordstream.com/blog/ws/2021/07/12/facebook-ads-cost

## Average Costs (2025 Data)

- Traffic campaigns: Average CPC $0.70
- Leads campaigns: Average CPC $1.92
- Average cost per lead: $27.66

## Industry Benchmarks

### Cheapest (Traffic):
- Shopping, Collectibles & Gifts: $0.34 CPC
- Sports & Recreation: $0.41 CPC

### Most Expensive (Traffic):
- Finance & Insurance: $1.22 CPC
- Home & Home Improvement: $0.99 CPC

### Leads CPC Range:
- Cheapest: Restaurants & Food ($0.74)
- Most Expensive: Dentists & Dental ($9.78)

## 7 Key Cost Factors

1. Campaign objectives: Awareness costs less than conversion-focused
2. Audience size: Larger audiences typically cost less
3. Daily budget: Lower budgets = higher costs during learning phase
4. Bidding strategy: Manual vs automated affects spending
5. Click-through rate: Healthy CTR (1-3%) = lower costs
6. Industry competition: Legal, dental services are pricier
7. Seasonality: Q4 costs increase due to holiday competition

## Cost Reduction Strategies

- Full-funnel approach: Start with awareness before conversion
- Broaden targeting to reduce per-click costs
- Check audience overlap to eliminate duplicate targeting
- Use bid caps based on conversion data
- Deploy both Pixel and Conversions API
- A/B test creative, copy, landing pages
- Leverage Meta Advantage+ for automation

## Facebook vs Google Ads

Facebook average CPC significantly lower than Google Ads ($5.26 CPC average), making it most cost-effective social advertising platform.
