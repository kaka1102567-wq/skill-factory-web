# Facebook Ad Objectives: Complete Guide (2025)

Source: https://www.wordstream.com/blog/facebook-ad-objectives

## The 6 Facebook Ad Objectives

### 1. Awareness
Maximize reach and brand visibility. Best for new brands, new markets, product launches. Combines former Brand Awareness, Reach, and Store Traffic.

### 2. Traffic
Drive clicks to website, landing page, or app. Best for blog content, e-commerce visits, app landing pages.

### 3. Engagement
Encourage interactions: likes, shares, comments, page likes, event responses. Best for video content, community building. Now includes former Messages, Conversions, Video Views.

### 4. Leads
Generate lead forms and inquiries. Two formats: instant forms (higher volume) or website conversions (better engagement). Best for signups, webinar registrations, ebook downloads.

### 5. Sales
Drive direct purchases. Best for product sales, promotions, catalog sales. Replaced former Conversions and Catalog Sales.

### 6. App Promotion
Generate app installs or re-engage existing users. Best for rapid installations and user acquisition.

## Key Changes (2023-2024)

Meta consolidated 11 original objectives into 6. All functionality still available under new names or as settings within other objectives. Legacy campaigns required conversion by end of 2024.
