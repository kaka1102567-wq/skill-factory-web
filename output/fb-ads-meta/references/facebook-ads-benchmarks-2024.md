# Facebook Ads Benchmarks 2024: Key Metrics

Source: https://www.wordstream.com/blog/facebook-ads-benchmarks-2024

## Overall Trends

CTR and conversion rates increased. CPC and CPL decreased across most industries.

## Traffic Campaign Benchmarks (All Industries)

- Average CTR: 1.57%
- Average CPC: $0.77

**Top performers:**
- Highest CTR: Real Estate (2.60%), Arts & Entertainment (2.59%)
- Lowest CPC: Travel ($0.42), Arts & Entertainment ($0.43)
- Highest CPC: Attorneys & Legal ($1.09)

## Leads Campaign Benchmarks (All Industries)

- Average CTR: 2.53%
- Average CPC: $1.88
- Average CVR: 8.78%
- Average CPL: $21.98

## Facebook vs Google Ads

Facebook: $1.88 CPC, $21.98 CPL average
Google Ads: $4.66 CPC, $66.69 CPL average
