# Facebook Ad Trends 2025

Source: https://www.wordstream.com/blog/facebook-ad-trends-2025

## 4 Key Trends

### 1. Highly Optimized Ad Creative is Essential
Users are savvier than ever. Use Canva, Meta's generative AI, and Meta Ads Library for compelling visuals.

### 2. Diversifying Creative Formats
Video priority: 86% of people want more brand videos. Best performing: 16-20 second videos. Reels and Stories ideal for mobile.

### 3. Prioritizing User Experience in Ad Formats
Native formats: lead gen forms with conditional logic, Instant Experiences, Messenger ads. Entire purchase journeys within the ad.

### 4. Leveraging Conversion Data Strategically
Target 30 conversions within 30 days to exit learning phase. Track earlier funnel actions when conversion volume is low.

## 7 Strategies to Outperform

1. Clear creative direction: Choose UGC style or premium polished videos
2. Test Advantage+ campaigns for automated optimization
3. Use Cost-Per-Result bidding with Campaign Budget Optimization
4. Implement broad targeting to gather optimization data
5. Avoid constant adjustments, especially early in campaigns
6. Diversify placements & formats (prioritize Reels for ecommerce)
7. Use 28-day click attribution window + Conversions API

## Additional Trends

- Short-form video: Facebook beats TikTok for US short-form consumption
- TikTok-style creative adoption across platforms
- Shoppable ads via Facebook Shops and Advantage+ Shopping campaigns
